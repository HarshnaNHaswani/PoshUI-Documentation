Badge

Badge can be used to show either status of the user (online, or offline) or you can use it show notification count.

Badge on Icons

                    
          <button class="badge-btn margin-lr-1">
            <i class="fas fa-home"></i>
            <span class="badge-count bg-amber">9</span>
          </button>
  
          <button class="badge-btn margin-lr-1">
            <i class="fas fa-shopping-cart"></i>
            <span class="badge-count bg-online">9+</span>
          </button>
  
          <button class="badge-btn margin-lr-1">
            <i class="fas fa-bell"></i>
            <span class="badge-count bg-dnd"> 99+ </span>
          </button>

Badge on Avatars

                    
          <div class="badge">
            <img class="avatar" src="..." alt="..." />        
            <span class="badge-status bg-online"></span>
          </div>

          <div class="badge">
            <img class="avatar" src="..." alt="..." />        
            <span class="badge-status bg-dnd"></span>
          </div>
  
          <div class="badge">
            <img class="avatar" src="..." alt="..." />
            <span class="badge-status bg-offline"></span>
          </div>
